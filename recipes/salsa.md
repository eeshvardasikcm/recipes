# Salsa

- 1 can (28 oz.) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
- ½ can of diced green chilies (or a jalapeno)
- 2 cloves garlic
- 4 scallions
- Handful fresh cilantro
- 1 tbsp. lime juice
- ½ tsp. chili powder
- ½ tsp. dried oregano
- Dash ground cumin
- Dash black pepper
- Salt to taste

Add all ingredients to blender. Blend until mostly smooth but still a bit chunky.

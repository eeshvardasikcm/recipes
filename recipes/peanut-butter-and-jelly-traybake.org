#+title: Peanut Butter & Jelly Traybake

Another recipe stolen from [[https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/recipes/peanut_butter_jelly_30293][Nadiya Hussain]].

- 250g all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp. baking powder
- ½ tsp. salt
- 3 tbsp. granulated sugar
- 170g milk (soy or almond are fine)
- 2 eggs
- 2 tbsp. vegetable oil
- 3 tbsp. peanut butter
- 3 tbsp. jam, any kind you'd like

Preheat oven to 350°.

Lightly oil a 9" baking pan.

Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a big bowl. Whisk in milk, eggs,
and oil to form a batter.

Pour batter into pan.

Stud the pan with dollops of peanut butter and jam, then swirl it all together
to marbled it.

Bake for about 15 min. Serve with powdered sugar, maple syrup, or whatever you'd
like.

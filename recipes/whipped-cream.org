#+title: Whipped Cream

- 1 pt. whipping cream (or heavy cream)
- 2 tbsp. confectioners' sugar
- 2 tsp. vanilla

If using a whipped cream dispenser, combine all ingredients in the canister,
shake well, charge, shake some more, and disperse.

Or, by hand, whip cream until stiff peaks form. Mix in sugar and vanilla. Serve.

#+title: Stir-fry Sauces

A few different ones.

/Basic sauce/

- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 tbsp. soy sauce
- 1 tbsp. rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp. brown sugar
- ½ c. vegetable stock
- 1½ tbsp. cornstarch

/Orange-ginger sauce/

- 1" ginger, minced
- 3 tbsp. soy sauce
- 1 tbsp. rice vinegar
- ½ c. orange juice (or, the juice of one orange)
- 1½ tbsp. cornstarch

/Black pepper sauce/

- 3 tbsp. soy sauce
- 2 tsp. rice vinegar
- 1 tsp. brown sugar
- ½ c. vegetable stock
- 1 tsp. black pepper
- 1 tbsp. cornstarch

/Sweet & sour sauce/

- 1 tbsp. soy sauce
- 2 tbsp. ketchup
- 1 tbsp. rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp. brown sugar
- ⅔ c. vegetable stock
- 1½ tbsp. cornstarch
